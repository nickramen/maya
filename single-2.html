San Pedro Sula City Highlights

San Pedro Sula isn't just a city — it's where modern Honduras pulses with energy while honoring its rich past. From a grand cathedral to a bustling market overflowing with handcrafted treasures, this tour reveals the soul of Honduras' industrial capital.

✦ Duration:
4 Hours
✦ Departures:
9:00 am & 2:00 pm

What You'll Experience

Cathedral & Main Square

Stand in the shadow of architectural grandeur where locals gather and history echoes through ancient stones.

Museum of Anthropology & History

Journey through millenia as expert curators unveil the story of Honduras, from pre-Columbian civilizations to the present day.

Guamilito Arts & Crafts Market

Lose yourself in a kaleidoscope of colors, where master artisans showcase generations of tradition in textiles, ceramics, and woodwork.

Authentic Flavors

Savor the rich complexity of Honduran chocolate or sip freshly brewed local coffee — each taste tells a story of the land.

Urban Exploration

Wind through vibrant commercial districts and peaceful residential neighborhoods, seeing how San Pedro Sula lives and breathes.

Complete Comfort

Professional tour escort, air-conditioned transportation, and all entrance fees covered — just bring your curiosity.
